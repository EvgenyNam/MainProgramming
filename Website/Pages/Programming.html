  • An arduino directional detection

I constructed the circuit using a few electret microphones and amplified their analog signal using LM386 opamp. The idea was that the microphone's whose readings went above a certain threshold, was the microphone that heard the noise first.

With 2 microphones, it is possible to determine the 'symmetrical' direction of sound. However due to the sample rate of arduino, the distance between microphones needs to be greater than ~1 meter.
